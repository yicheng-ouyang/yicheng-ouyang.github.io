The Hook Man (2018)

It is a vertical-scroll game inspired by Doodle Jump. In this game you can control a ninja to climb higher and higher by using a Grappling Hook! Try not to fall, or your ninja may die!

Smartphone：Use the virtual joystick to control the direction, use the buttons to accelerate horizontally and launch the grappling hook.

PC：A/D key to accelerate horizontally, space to launch the grappling hook. Use mouse to control the virtual joystick to adjust direction.
